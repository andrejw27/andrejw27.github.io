About Me

I am a passionate Data Scientist with hands-on experience in Recommender Systems and a dedicated PhD student, specializing in the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and public health. My research focuses on leveraging advanced machine learning techniques to address critical challenges in public health and contribute to the well-being of communities.
